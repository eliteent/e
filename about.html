Our Story

Founded in 2014 by three operators, not academics

Elite Consulting Group started when our founders — a former VP of Operations, a CFO, and a growth marketer — kept getting hired to "fix" strategies that were technically correct and practically unusable. So they built a firm with one rule: every recommendation has to survive contact with a Monday morning team meeting.

A decade later, that rule still governs every engagement. We've grown to a team of 40+ senior consultants working across 12 industries, but we've kept the firm intentionally boutique — every client works directly with a partner-level consultant, never a rotating cast of junior analysts.
