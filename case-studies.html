Retail · Operations Consulting

Brightfield Retail cuts fulfillment time 52%

40-store regional retailer, 1,200 employees

The Challenge

Brightfield's order fulfillment times had crept up as online orders grew past 30% of revenue, causing missed delivery windows and rising customer complaints.

The Solution

We mapped the fulfillment process across 8 pilot stores, identified redundant handling steps, and redesigned staffing and inventory placement around peak order windows.

Implementation

A 10-week pilot across 8 stores was followed by a phased rollout to all 40 locations over 4 months, with weekly performance check-ins.

"We didn't just get a report — Elite's consultants were on the floor with our store managers until the new process stuck."

Hospitality · HR & Leadership Coaching

Harborview Hotels cuts management turnover 40%

Boutique hotel group, 6 properties

The Challenge

Harborview was losing property managers faster than it could train replacements, driven by inconsistent leadership support and unclear career paths.

The Solution

We redesigned the management career ladder, introduced compensation benchmarking, and rolled out monthly leadership coaching for all property managers.

Implementation

A 4-month HR redesign followed by an ongoing 6-month coaching program across all six properties.
